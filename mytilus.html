MYTILUS

When I had to think about what I could focus my final degree project on, I thought I could do something related to the sea and mussel farming, which is what my family does. That's how Mytilus was born, a project through which I try to predict the appearance of toxins in the Galician estuaries to improve production and make it more efficient and profitable.
